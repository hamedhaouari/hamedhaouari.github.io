Work


Focusing on business analysis, I developed the ability of clearly identifying needs or problems, exploring different solutions, detecting opportunities and preventing future needs and or problems.

Mastering object-oriented design and programming. I use JAVA EE (Hibernate JPA Spring Jsf) ,Java Fx and Web MVC frameworks such as CakePHP and YII2 besides client side technologies like HTML5 , CSS3,Bootstrap CSS, JQuery.

Inspired by the BI concepts I give a big attention to Data mining, dashboards using Jasper Reports and Ui libraries like Primefaces jsf library and JQuery UI. As a fun of Mathematical economics I insist to produce effective reports based on the right statistics.

Working with data I master SQL relational databases like mysql and Oracle database, NoSql databases such as CouchDB and MongoDB. And I use Talend for ETL operations.

I have a solid understanding of data processing with scala and of Hadoop’s ecosystem ( HDFS, MapReduce, Hive, Oozie, Flume, Pig, YARN, and HBase) developpement, configuration and installation.
